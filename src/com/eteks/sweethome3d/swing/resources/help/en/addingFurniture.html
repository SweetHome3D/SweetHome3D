Sweet Home 3D Help > Creating homes, walls and furniture
     Adding furniture
 

To add furniture to your home, drag and drop one or more pieces of furniture from catalog to home plan or furniture list.

You may also select some pieces in catalog, then choose Furniture > Add to home or click on Add to home tool.

 Add to home tool

When pieces are dropped in plan, the location of their top left corner is at the point where you release the mouse button.
When pieces are dropped in furniture list or added with Furniture > Add to home menu, the location of their top left corner is at the point (0, 0).

The pieces added to home are selected and drawn simultaneously in furniture list, in plan and in the 3D view. During the loading time of the 3D model of added pieces, these pieces are represented as a white box in the 3D view.

•

If the catalog doesn't contain the piece of furniture or the object you want to add to your home, choose the Box piece in the Miscellaneous category and resize it, or import it from an other furniture library.

•

A piece of furniture qualified as a door or a window indicates to Sweet Home 3D to automatically make holes in walls at the intersection location with doors or windows. This computation isn't made for the other kinds of furniture.

•

Once a piece is added to home, you may change its name, its angle, its location, its size and its color.

•

Choose Plan > Zoom in or Plan > Zoom out to change plan scale.

•

Select tool is automatically selected once you added a piece of furniture to home.
