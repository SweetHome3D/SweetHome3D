     Editing walls
 

You may edit the location and length of home walls, either with the mouse or with Plan > Modify walls... menu.
To edit walls with the mouse, select them in home plan first. To select one wall in plan, click on it. To select multiple walls in plan, draw a selection rectangle that contains them, or click on walls while pressing shift key.
To move the selected walls, place the mouse inside the selected box of one these walls then drag and drop them, or press the keyboard arrows.

When one wall is selected in plan, you may also move its start and end point, with the size indicator that appear at each end of the selected wall.

1.

The start point size indicator shows the point you can drag and drop to move the start point of the wall.

2.

The wall start indicator shows from which point starts a wall.

3.

The wall end indicator shows at which point ends a wall.

4.

The end point size indicator shows the point you can drag and drop to move the end point of the wall.

When the mouse pointer is upon the start or end point of the selected wall, it changes to indicate you can drag and drop that point to move it. While you press the mouse button, a tooltip shows the length of the wall.
The walls modified with the mouse are drawn simultaneously in plan and in the 3D view.

A wall may also be edited thanks to its pane, by double-clicking on that wall in home plan, or by choosing Plan > Modify walls... after selecting it.

In the wall pane, you may change the coordinates of its start and end point, the colors of its left and right sides, its thickness and its height.
Use the wall start and wall end indicators drawn in plan on each selected wall to distinguish its left side from its right side.

•

If you want to modify the colors, the thickness or the height of a set of walls, select these walls and choose Furniture > Modify.... The values of the attributes that are different from one wall to another in the selected walls set, will appear blank in the walls pane.

•

If you want to set different colors on the side of a wall shared by adjacent rooms, divide it in multiple segments at its creation.

•

If plan selected items contain walls and furniture, all these items will be moved with a drag and drop or the keyboard arrows.

•

The unit displayed by default in length tooltip and wall pane depends on your country. You may switch to Centimeter or Inch in the preferences pane.

•

Choose Plan > Zoom in or Plan > Zoom out to change plan scale.
